SOUNDISLANDS 2017

  1. HOME
  2. NEWS
  3. SOUNDISLANDS 2017

Posted on April 25, 2017 at 7:05 PM

SELCT researchers Jon He, Bridget Johnson, and Jim Murphy performed and presented at Soundislands 2017 at the Arts House in Singpore from 5-10 April, 2017. This event, organised by PerMagnus Lindborg through NTU, consisted of film showings, installations, performances, presentations, and panel discussions.

To go with the festival's launch, Jim installed Orrery 1 and Orrery 2 (shown in this post's title image). These kinetic sculptures accompanied AXIS, a film directed by Daniel Belton of Good Company Arts. Jim's Orrery sculptures were fabricated at SELCT's facilities and consist of motorised coaxial armitures programmed to rotate in opposing directions at subtly differing speeds.

Bridget, Jon, and Jim collaborated on an extended performance featuring Swivel 2, speaker.motion, and g.qin. This performance (audiovisual documentation forthcoming) served as a structured improvisatory presentation of some of SELCT's showcase projects.

The festival concluded with talks and panels; Jim and Bridget contributed to these, describing the process that led to the previous evening's collaborative piece. The theme for Soundislands 2017 was 'sound and movement,' and the strong presence of SELCT's kinetic, performative, and mechatronic devices led to compelling discussions about mechanical movement and its role in dynamic artforms.

More information about the festival may be found at the Soundislands homepage.
