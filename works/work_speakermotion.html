ABOUT

speaker.motion is a mechatronic loudspeaker system developed by SELCT PhD alumnus Bridget Johnson. Each of the four speaker.motion modules may be aimed with MIDI commands, allowing composers and musicians to precisely direct sonic content to different areas of a concert or installation venue.
