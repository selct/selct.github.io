SELCT RESEARCHERS

SELCT consists of faculty and students who work across a range of audio-based fields. As an interdisciplinary research group, SELCT contains researchers from composition, music technology, and engineering backgrounds.

The diverse range of interests and areas of expertise among SELCT researchers paves the way for a wide range of inventive collaborations: postgraduate students may choose to work with any of the SELCT faculty, allowing for innovative fusions of sub-disciplines.

In addition to the SELCT members listed below, SELCT engages with a worldwide array of collaborators and colleagues.
