SELCT is actively seeking researchers who are interested in working at the junction of engineering and sonic arts. SELCT's research focuses on sound art and sculpture, applied music information retrieval, musical mechatronics, and new musical interface design.

Those seeking to pursue postgraduate studies at the doctorate or masters level should first contact one of the SELCT faculty: Jim Murphy, Dale A. Carnegie, Ajay Kapur, Mo H. Zareei, Michael Norris, and Dugal McKinnon. In your introductory email, please include a brief mention of your areas of interest as well as a CV.
